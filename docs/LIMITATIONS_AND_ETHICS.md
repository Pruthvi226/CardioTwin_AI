# Limitations And Ethics

- Research demo only. Not for clinical diagnosis or medical decision-making.
- Synthetic data validates software flow only, not real-world clinical performance.
- Real-world validation requires governed datasets, subject-wise splits, bias analysis, and clinical review.
- Sensor noise, motion artifacts, skin tone, physiology, device type, and population shift can affect model behavior.
- Low-confidence or low-quality predictions should require human review.
